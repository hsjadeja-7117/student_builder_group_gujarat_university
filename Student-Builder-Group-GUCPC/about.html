Our story

From a cloud interest group to a full builder community

Student Builder Group began as a small circle of students at GUCPC experimenting with cloud platforms after class. As more students asked to join — from web development, design, and data — the group grew into something broader: a home for anyone who wants to build, regardless of where they're starting from.

Today, SBG runs regular workshops, speaker sessions, and build nights, backed by a student-led core team and a growing network of volunteers and mentors.

What we do

Four ways we bring the community together

Workshops

Hands-on sessions on cloud, web, and dev tools — built for beginners and repeatable for anyone.

Build nights

Short, focused sprints where small teams ship a working prototype in a single evening.

Speaker sessions

Engineers and alumni sharing what actually happens once a project reaches production.

Mentorship

Core team members and volunteers pairing with newer members on real, ongoing projects.
