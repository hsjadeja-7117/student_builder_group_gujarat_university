Our mission

To give every student at GUCPC a front-row seat to how real technology actually works — moving past theory in isolation to understand how the industry builds at scale.

How we started

Sparked within the GUCPC cloud community, Student Builder Group launched as its own chapter to give curious minds across every discipline a unified home on campus.

◆

Where we're headed

Bigger speaker sessions, stronger core mentorship, and a campus culture where industry insights are accessible to everyone..

Our story

From a cloud interest group to a full builder community

Student Builder Group began as a small circle of students at GUCPC experimenting with cloud platforms after class. As more students asked to join — from web development, design, Cyber Security, and data — the group grew into something broader: a home for anyone who wants to build, regardless of where they're starting from.

Today, SBG connects students directly with industry professionals through expert talks and Q&As, supported by core team dedicated to guiding campus talent.

What we do

Four ways we bring the community together

Industry Keynotes

Direct talks from field professionals and engineers sharing how real-world systems run at scale.

Build nights

Conceptual breakdowns of modern technologies, architectures, and tools used beyond the classroom.

Career & Production Insights

Alumni and industry guests sharing what actually happens when tech goes live—from incidents to career growth.

Core Mentorship

Core team members and volunteers pairing with newer members on offering guidance, answering questions, and helping with navigating the tech path.
